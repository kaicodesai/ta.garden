Booking & Cancellation Policy

Everything you need to know, written plainly. If you have questions, just ask us.

Security Deposit

Your Security Deposit

When you confirm a booking, a refundable security deposit equal to 50% of one month's rent is required within 72 hours to hold your room. If payment is not received within 72 hours, the room is released.

The deposit is held during your stay and returned to you within 7 days of checkout, provided the room is in good condition. You will need to provide an electronic payment method — PayPal, Wise, or similar — to receive your refund.

⚠️ If there is damage to the room beyond normal wear and tear, we may partially or fully withhold the deposit. We will always provide photo evidence of any damage before making a deduction.

Before Move-In

Cancelling Before You Arrive

If you need to cancel before your move-in date, the following applies to your security deposit:

30+ days before move-in
Full deposit refunded
14–29 days before move-in
50% of deposit refunded
Under 14 days before move-in
Deposit non-refundable
No-show (no contact)
Deposit non-refundable

If you have already paid your first month's rent and then cancel:

When you cancelWhat happens to first month's rent

Under 14 days before move-inFirst month's rent fully refunded — only the deposit is kept
Last-Minute Bookings

Booking Within 14 Days of Move-In

If your move-in date is within 14 days of when you book, your deposit is non-refundable from the moment of booking. This is because we have very little time to find a replacement guest if you cancel.

We will always tell you this clearly before you pay. You will see a notice in your booking confirmation and on the payment screen. We want you to feel confident before committing — if you have any doubts, please reach out first.

If you have extenuating circumstances (see Force Majeure below), we will always consider your situation with care.

After Move-In

Leaving Early or Ending Your Stay

If you decide to leave before your agreed move-out date, we ask for 7 days written notice. Written notice means a WhatsApp message, email, or any written communication sent directly to us — the 7 days starts from the moment we receive it.

SituationWhat happens
You give 7 days written noticeYou pay through the end of the 7-day notice period (prorated if mid-month). Deposit returned within 7 days of checkout.
You leave physically before the notice period endsYou are still liable for rent through the full 7-day notice period.
Partial last monthYou only pay for the days you stay through the end of the notice period — calculated daily.
Exceptional Circumstances

Force Majeure

Life happens. If you need to cancel due to a serious illness, visa denial, family emergency, or other circumstances genuinely outside your control, we will issue a full refund of your deposit and any rent paid — at our discretion, with supporting documentation (medical certificate, visa rejection letter, etc.).

Please contact us as soon as possible if you find yourself in this situation. We will always try to find a fair solution.

If We Cancel

Host Cancellation

If Ta.Garden needs to cancel your booking for any reason, you will receive a full refund of your deposit and any rent paid, promptly and without question.
